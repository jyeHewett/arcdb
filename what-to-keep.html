ARC Raiders Essential Items: What to Keep

In ARC Raiders, keeping the right items is crucial for progression. This guide identifies the essential items you should always keep in your inventory, organized by importance and use case.

Always Keep - Top Priority Items

  • Arc Alloy - High-value crafting component used in advanced recipes. Keep minimum 10-20 units.
  • Arc Circuitry - Essential for electronic crafting. Always keep available stock.
  • Arc Powercells - Used in many advanced recipes and modifications. Never sell these.
  • Advanced Arc Powercell - Even rarer than standard powercells, critical for top-tier crafting.
  • Items marked "Keep for Quests/Workshop" - Required for story/event progression and crafting.

Important Crafting Materials to Stockpile

  • Arc Performance Steel - High-tier material for weapon and armor crafting
  • Arc Synthetic Resin - Needed for multiple workshop recipes
  • Arc Flex Rubber - Used in equipment modifications
  • Arc Motion Core - Rare component for advanced items
  • Electrical/Mechanical Components (Advanced) - Mid-to-high tier crafting essentials

How to Identify Items to Keep in ARC Raiders

Use the searchable database above to quickly check any item. Look for these indicators that an item should be kept:

  • "Keep for Quests/Workshop" flag - The most important indicator
  • Uncommon or Rare rarity - Higher rarity often means harder to find
  • "Needed For" field populated - Item is used in crafting recipes
  • High sell price (500+) - Even if you can sell it, consider keeping for later
  • Arc-prefixed items - Almost all Arc materials are valuable

ARC Raiders Quest and Workshop Items

Many items in ARC Raiders are specifically needed for quest completion or workshop crafting. These items show specific quest/workshop counts in the database:

  • Quest items with specific location requirements (keys, access cards)
  • Workshop materials showing counts like "18x Workshop 80x Expedition"
  • Components from named enemies (Hornet Driver, Wasp Driver, etc.)
  • Special cells and cores (Bastion Cell, Queen Reactor, etc.)

Always check the database before selling to see if an item has quest or workshop requirements.

Quick Priority System for ARC Raiders Inventory

When inventory is tight, use this priority system to decide what to keep:

  1. Priority 1: Items flagged for Quests/Workshop - never sell
  2. Priority 2: Arc-prefixed materials and advanced components
  3. Priority 3: Rare components from named enemies
  4. Priority 4: Uncommon crafting materials you use frequently
  5. Priority 5: Common materials (keep 5-10 of each)

ARC Raiders Material Stocking Recommendations

Optimal inventory levels for common materials in ARC Raiders:

  • Arc Alloy: 10-20 units
  • Arc Circuitry: 5-15 units
  • Arc Powercells: All you can get
  • Metal Parts: 20-50 units
  • Electrical Components: 10-30 units
  • Mechanical Components: 10-30 units
  • Common materials: 5-10 units each
