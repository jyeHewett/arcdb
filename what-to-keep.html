What to Keep in ARC Raiders

Short answer: prioritize rare crafting components, Arc Powercells, and any item flagged in-game as "Keep for Quests/Workshop" — these are used in high-value recipes and quest progress.

Quick checklist — keep these

  • Arc Alloy / Arc Circuitry — high-value crafting components
  • Arc Powercells — used in many advanced recipes
  • Items marked "Keep for Quests/Workshop" — required for story/event progression
  • Rare components from named enemies or high-tier loot sources

How to prioritize

When inventory is tight, first keep anything rare or required by current workshop recipes and quests. Sell common materials you have in bulk and items not listed as keep.

Quick reference

Use item detail pages on this site to check the "Keep for Quests/Workshop" flag — if present, keep the item. Otherwise consider rarity and sell price.
