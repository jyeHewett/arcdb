ARC Raiders Selling Strategy: What to Sell First

Managing your inventory in ARC Raiders is crucial for success. Knowing what to sell helps you maximize profits while keeping essential items for crafting and quests. Here's your complete guide to selling items efficiently in ARC Raiders.

  • Common crafting materials - If you have large stacks (20+) of common materials, sell excess amounts
  • Duplicate low-rarity items - Items not used in recipes and not flagged for quests
  • Items with low sell price and no 'Keep' flag - Safe to sell when you need space
  • Damaged or broken items - Unless needed for specific recipes, these can usually be sold
  • Decorative items - Snow globes, photographs, and collectibles without quest markers

ARC Raiders Best Items to Sell for Profit

Some items in ARC Raiders have high sell prices but aren't essential for progression. Focus on selling these high-value items that aren't marked for quests or workshops:

  • Items with sell prices over 500 that have no crafting use
  • Rare decorative items found in the world
  • Excess keys after you've unlocked their corresponding areas
  • Enemy-specific drops you don't need for current recipes

When NOT to Sell Items in ARC Raiders

Do not sell items flagged as 'Keep for Quests/Workshop' or rare components needed for crafting advanced gear. Before selling any item in ARC Raiders, check this database's item details to verify it's not needed for:

  • Active or future quest requirements
  • Workshop crafting recipes you plan to use
  • Arc Powercells, Arc Alloy, Arc Circuitry - essential high-tier materials
  • Any item with "needed_for" crafting relationships

ARC Raiders Inventory Management Tips

Efficient inventory management in ARC Raiders means knowing what to keep and what to sell. Follow these tips:

  • Regularly check your inventory against this database before vendor trips
  • Sort items by sell price in the database to identify high-value sellables
  • Keep at least 5-10 of any common crafting material for emergencies
  • Sell duplicates of unique items once you've completed their associated content
  • Use the search function above to quickly find specific items
